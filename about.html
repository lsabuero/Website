About Uruk Group

Iraq's Engineering Excellence

Founded in 2003, Uruk Group has emerged as Iraq's leading engineering and EPC company, dedicated to rebuilding the nation's infrastructure and creating a sustainable future.

With over two decades of experience, our team of expert engineers and project managers have successfully delivered critical infrastructure projects across the energy, water, and transportation sectors throughout the Middle East.

Combining local expertise with international standards, Uruk Group bridges the gap between global engineering practices and the unique challenges of the Iraqi market.

Our Journey

2003

Formation of Uruk Group

Established as a private enterprise with the mandate to offer Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) services.

2005

First Major Project

Successful completion of the company's first major power plant project, demonstrating Uruk's engineering expertise.

2010

International Expansion

Established partnerships with global engineering firms and opened offices in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.

2015

Major Infrastructure Projects

Secured contracts for critical national infrastructure projects including the Taji Power Plant.

2020

Innovation Initiative

Launched research and development division focused on sustainable engineering solutions.

Today

Leading the Future

Continuing to shape Iraq's infrastructure landscape with cutting-edge engineering and commitment to excellence.
