About Uruk Group

Our Story

Uruk Group was founded in 2003 by Iraqi engineers and entrepreneurs with a vision to rebuild and modernize Iraq's energy and infrastructure sectors. Over two decades, we have delivered landmark projects in partnership with global leaders, always upholding the highest standards of safety, quality, and innovation.

Our Mission

To empower Iraq's future through world-class engineering, procurement, and construction services, blending local expertise with international best practices.

Our Values

  • Integrity & Transparency
  • Innovation & Excellence
  • Safety & Sustainability
  • Collaboration & Partnership
