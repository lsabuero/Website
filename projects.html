Building Iraq's Energy Future

Project Excellence

From power plants to oil infrastructure, Uruk delivers world-class engineering solutions across Iraq.

Uruk's senior management have been involved in Iraq's most important energy projects of the last three decades, including the nation's nuclear program and electricity plant construction and rehabilitation. Our team have participated either on a turnkey engineering contract, as project or construction managers, or simply procurement and manpower specialists.
